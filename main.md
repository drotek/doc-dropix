#Main Devices

Some devices are **mandatory** in order to get your drone to fly using the Dropix autopilot. Here is a quick list of these necessary items: 

- ESC / Motor couple
- Arming switch
- Buzzer
- LED Module
- Power Management Unit
- Radio receiver
